# Jeffin with Godot

A fast-paced mini-game collection built in Godot 4. Test your reaction speed and mash your way through micro-challenges.

---

## How to Play

1. Hub/Map: Press SPACE to enter a random mini-game!
2. Complete the challenge before the timer runs out or before you fall.
3. Win to see the victory screen, or lose and face the death screen!

---

## Included Mini-Games

### 1. Parkour Runner
* Goal: Jump across randomly generated platforms and reach the end goal line.
* Danger: Don't drop into the lava below!
* Controls: Movement & Jump keys.

### 2. Space Masher
* Goal: Rapidly mash the SPACE key 10 times within 5 seconds.
* Controls: SPACE key.

---

## Built With

* Engine: Godot 4
* Language: GDScript
